Share media


Glue is an event directory made by you with shared media & social data.

You enrich the event with your content: you can share your thoughts, your ideas and your opinions, and you can add pictures, videos and sounds.

Thus, you become a full participant by helping organizers to shape the event.

Great mobile experience


Contribute to an event in real-time.
This is now made possible through the emerging 4G system providing mobile ultra-broadband Internet access, and the increasing number of mobile devices with an advanced operating system (smartphones, tablets but also connected cameras ).

Our vision of what it should be! The challenges ahead.

We don't want Glue to be a simple mashup that only aggregates content from multiple social networks. This would reflect individual behaviors too much (selfies, raw collection of personal opinions, meaningless user whereabouts). Instead, we want to give you great overviews of events. It is important to make data more useful and more structured, with an actual content of interest.

Towards a Data Heaven

For doing this, we count on you to make Glue a real community platform, maintained collectively, by leveraging our mass collaboration tools and moderating any other user's contributions .
As for ourselves, we put a great emphasis on Open Data to offer you fresh and abundant information, and the API will allow developers to imagine new innovative services. In the long term, we aim to deal with semantization of data and we will gradually move on to Semantic Web technologies...

We aim to build a collective memory, to allow you to Discover, Live & Relive Past, Present and Future events.

At last, we want to make Glue a large-scale service available everywhere, but offering localized event data that fits you, the end user.
